Katie makes a green smoothie almost every morning. She and her husband Brian love to share the bright green drink in their bright green kitchen each morning, alongside a handful of almonds and a teaspoon of cod liver oil.

Since 2009, Katie's green smoothie enthusiasm has flourished after learning this special recipe from Josh Whiton. They both agree, the world needs more leafy greens.

As an after school teacher, an acrobat with AcroEntertainment, and an avid supporter of organic, local, seasonal foods, she urges others to share in the green smoothie revolution!

Feel energized in the mornings and rejuvenated in the evenings!

Join us in making the world a better place, by drinking a green smoothie! Please log your green smoothie consumption on this website and help us spread the word!
